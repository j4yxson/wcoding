Margin vs Border vs Padding


CSS Box Model

What is a box model?

A box model is essentially a box that wraps around every piece of HTML content, consisting of a margin, a border, and padding


What is the difference between Margin and Border and Padding?

Margin - the space outside of an element's border
Border - a border around an element
Padding - the space in between the border and the content
